Who We Are

Cockroach Army is a youth-driven social initiative that believes in the power of collective action. Like cockroaches — resilient, relentless, and impossible to ignore — we tackle the issues that others overlook.

From cleaning neglected streets to raising awareness about corruption, from promoting health education to empowering underprivileged communities — we show up, we act, and we never back down.

Our Missions

Six pillars that drive everything we do. Each mission is a battle we fight every single day.

🌿 Environment Protection

Tree plantation drives, plastic-free campaigns, and wildlife conservation efforts across cities and villages.

⚖️ Anti-Corruption

Spreading awareness about citizens' rights, RTI filing assistance, and building transparency in public systems.

🧹 Cleanliness Drives

Regular clean-up drives in public spaces, beaches, rivers, and neighbourhood areas to create cleaner communities.

❤️ Health Awareness

Free health camps, blood donation drives, mental health awareness sessions, and hygiene education programs.

📚 Education for All

Free tutoring for underprivileged children, scholarship guidance, digital literacy workshops, and school supply drives.

💼 Employment Support

Skill development workshops, resume building, interview preparation, and connecting youth with job opportunities.
